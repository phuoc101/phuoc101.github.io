Hi! I am **Phuoc**, a doctoral researcher at the [Intelligent Robotics Group](https://irobotics.aalto.fi) at Aalto University, Finland. I am supervised by [Prof. Ville Kyrki](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=8OBnyXQAAAAJ&hl=fi&oi=ao) and advised by [Dr. Francesco Verdoja](https://fverdoja.github.io/). My research interests are **Robotic Semantic Scene Understanding, Robotics Mapping and Machine Learning**. My goal is to create representations of our world that enable robots to understand our human-centric environment and assist humans in our daily lives.

Previously, I received my B.Sc degree in Mechatronic Engineering from Ho Chi Minh University of Technology, Vietnam. After that, I pursued my M.Sc(Tech) degree in Robotics and AI from Tampere University, Finland. During my second year, I worked as a research assistant at the [Turku Intelligent Embedded and Robotic Systems (TIERS)](tiers.utu.fi) of the University of Turku.
